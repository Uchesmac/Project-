African Food Recipes

Jollof Rice

A popular West African dish that is flavorful and aromatic.

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper, chopped (optional for heat)
  • 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Samu Recipe (Ethiopian Lentil Stew)

A delicious and hearty dish from Ethiopia, rich in flavor and protein.

  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Bobotie

A South African dish with spiced minced meat and an egg-based topping.

  • 1 pound ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 slices of bread, soaked in milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 2 tablespoons chutney
  • 1/4 cup almonds, slivered
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
